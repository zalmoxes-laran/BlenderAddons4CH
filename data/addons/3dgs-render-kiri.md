The recommended current 3DGS tool. Imports, edits and renders 3DGS `.ply`,
integrates with **EEVEE** (the only add-on that does), supports rigging splats
from a proxy mesh and exporting animated 4DGS as `.ply` sequences (v5.x).

Free, GPL-3.0, Blender 5.1+.
